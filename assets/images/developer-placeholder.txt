# Professional Developer Image

To add your professional photo:

1. Save your professional headshot as "developer.jpg" in this folder
2. Recommended image specifications:
   - Size: 400x400 pixels (square format)
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - Quality: High resolution
   - Style: Professional headshot with neutral background
   - Dress: Business casual or formal attire

3. Alternative: Use a professional photo service like:
   - Unsplash: https://unsplash.com/s/photos/professional-developer
   - Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/search/professional%20developer/
   - Or take a professional headshot

4. The image will automatically display once you add "developer.jpg" to this folder

Current placeholder will show until you add your image.
